Smoothie Milkshake

Frozen ingredients:
  • Strawberries
  • Blueberries
  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Ice cubes
Procedure:
  1. 1. Combine ingredients in a blender.
  2. 2. Blend until texture is calm.
  3. 3. Serve in a mason jar.
Ingredients:
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Raspberry jam
Optional: Top with marshmallows.
